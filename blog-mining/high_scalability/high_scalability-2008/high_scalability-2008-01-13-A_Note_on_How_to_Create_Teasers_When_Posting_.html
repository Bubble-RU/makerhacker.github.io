A teaser is a paragraph interesting enough to convince readers to click on the "read more" link to get the full article.breakCreating a teaser in Drupal is accomplished by inserting < ! -- break -- > on a separate line directly after the text you want to be the teaser. Only DO NOT include the spaces. So your post looks like:Teaser Content< ! -- break -- > (no spaces in real life)Rest of ContentIt's a bit kludgey, but it works.
