Source: pdf

Author: Abhinav Gupta, Martial Hebert, Takeo Kanade, David M. Blei

Abstract: There has been a recent push in extraction of 3D spatial layout of scenes. However, none of these approaches model the 3D interaction between objects and the spatial layout. In this paper, we argue for a parametric representation of objects in 3D, which allows us to incorporate volumetric constraints of the physical world.
