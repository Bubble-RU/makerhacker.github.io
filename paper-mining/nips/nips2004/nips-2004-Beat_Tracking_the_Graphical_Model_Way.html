29 nips-2004-Beat Tracking the Graphical Model Way


Source: pdf

Author: Dustin Lang, Nando D. Freitas

Abstract: We present a graphical model for beat tracking in recorded music. Using a probabilistic graphical model allows us to incorporate local information and global smoothness constraints in a principled manner. We evaluate our model on a set of varied and difÔ¨Åcult examples, and achieve impressive results. By using a fast dual-tree algorithm for graphical model inference, our system runs in less time than the duration of the music being processed. 1
