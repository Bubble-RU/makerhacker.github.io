100 nips-2004-Learning Preferences for Multiclass Problems


Source: pdf

Author: Fabio Aiolli, Alessandro Sperduti

Abstract: Many interesting multiclass problems can be cast in the general framework of label ranking deÔ¨Åned on a given set of classes. The evaluation for such a ranking is generally given in terms of the number of violated order constraints between classes. In this paper, we propose the Preference Learning Model as a unifying framework to model and solve a large class of multiclass problems in a large margin perspective. In addition, an original kernel-based method is proposed and evaluated on a ranking dataset with state-of-the-art results. 1
