181 nips-2003-Statistical Debugging of Sampled Programs


Source: pdf

Author: Alice X. Zheng, Michael I. Jordan, Ben Liblit, Alex Aiken

Abstract: We present a novel strategy for automatically debugging programs given sampled data from thousands of actual user runs. Our goal is to pinpoint those features that are most correlated with crashes. This is accomplished by maximizing an appropriately deÔ¨Åned utility function. It has analogies with intuitive debugging heuristics, and, as we demonstrate, is able to deal with various types of bugs that occur in real programs. 1
