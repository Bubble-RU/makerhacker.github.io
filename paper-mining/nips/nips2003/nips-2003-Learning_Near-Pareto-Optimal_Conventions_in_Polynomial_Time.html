105 nips-2003-Learning Near-Pareto-Optimal Conventions in Polynomial Time


Source: pdf

Author: Xiaofeng Wang, Tuomas Sandholm

Abstract: We study how to learn to play a Pareto-optimal strict Nash equilibrium when there exist multiple equilibria and agents may have different preferences among the equilibria. We focus on repeated coordination games of non-identical interest where agents do not know the game structure up front and receive noisy payoffs. We design efÔ¨Åcient near-optimal algorithms for both the perfect monitoring and the imperfect monitoring setting(where the agents only observe their own payoffs and the joint actions). 1
