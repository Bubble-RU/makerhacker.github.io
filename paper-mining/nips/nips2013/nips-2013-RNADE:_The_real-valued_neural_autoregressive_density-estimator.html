260 nips-2013-RNADE: The real-valued neural autoregressive density-estimator


Source: pdf

Author: Benigno Uria, Iain Murray, Hugo Larochelle

Abstract: We introduce RNADE, a new model for joint density estimation of real-valued vectors. Our model calculates the density of a datapoint as the product of onedimensional conditionals modeled using mixture density networks with shared parameters. RNADE learns a distributed representation of the data, while having a tractable expression for the calculation of densities. A tractable likelihood allows direct comparison with other methods and training by standard gradientbased optimizers. We compare the performance of RNADE on several datasets of heterogeneous and perceptual data, Ô¨Ånding it outperforms mixture models in all but one case. 1
