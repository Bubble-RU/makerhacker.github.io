Author: Alexander Rakhlin, Karthik Sridharan, Ambuj Tewari

Abstract: We develop a theory of online learning by deÔ¨Åning several complexity measures. Among them are analogues of Rademacher complexity, covering numbers and fatshattering dimension from statistical learning theory.
