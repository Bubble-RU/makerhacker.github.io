158 nips-2010-Learning via Gaussian Herding


Source: pdf

Author: Koby Crammer, Daniel D. Lee

Abstract: We introduce a new family of online learning algorithms based upon constraining the velocity ï¬‚ow over a distribution of weight vectors. In particular, we show how to effectively herd a Gaussian weight vector distribution by trading off velocity constraints with a loss function. By uniformly bounding this loss function, we demonstrate how to solve the resulting optimization analytically. We compare the resulting algorithms on a variety of real world datasets, and demonstrate how these algorithms achieve state-of-the-art robust performance, especially with high label noise in the training data. 1
