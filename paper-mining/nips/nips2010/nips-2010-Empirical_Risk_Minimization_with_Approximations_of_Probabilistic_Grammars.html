75 nips-2010-Empirical Risk Minimization with Approximations of Probabilistic Grammars


Source: pdf

Author: Noah A. Smith, Shay B. Cohen

Abstract: Probabilistic grammars are generative statistical models that are useful for compositional and sequential structures. We present a framework, reminiscent of structural risk minimization, for empirical risk minimization of the parameters of a Ô¨Åxed probabilistic grammar using the log-loss. We derive sample complexity bounds in this framework that apply both to the supervised setting and the unsupervised setting. 1
