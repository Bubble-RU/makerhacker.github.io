319 nips-2012-Sparse Prediction with the $k$-Support Norm


Source: pdf

Author: Andreas Argyriou, Rina Foygel, Nathan Srebro

Abstract: We derive a novel norm that corresponds to the tightest convex relaxation of sparsity combined with an 2 penalty. We show that this new k-support norm provides a tighter relaxation than the elastic net and can thus be advantageous in in sparse prediction problems. We also bound the looseness of the elastic net, thus shedding new light on it and providing justiÔ¨Åcation for its use. 1
