355 nips-2012-Truncation-free Online Variational Inference for Bayesian Nonparametric Models


Source: pdf

Author: Chong Wang, David M. Blei

Abstract: We present a truncation-free stochastic variational inference algorithm for Bayesian nonparametric models. While traditional variational inference algorithms require truncations for the model or the variational distribution, our method adapts model complexity on the ï¬‚y. We studied our method with Dirichlet process mixture models and hierarchical Dirichlet process topic models on two large data sets. Our method performs better than previous stochastic variational inference algorithms. 1
