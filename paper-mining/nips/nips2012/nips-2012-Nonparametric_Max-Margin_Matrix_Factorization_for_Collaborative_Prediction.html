246 nips-2012-Nonparametric Max-Margin Matrix Factorization for Collaborative Prediction


Source: pdf

Author: Minjie Xu, Jun Zhu, Bo Zhang

Abstract: We present a probabilistic formulation of max-margin matrix factorization and build accordingly a nonparametric Bayesian model which automatically resolves the unknown number of latent factors. Our work demonstrates a successful example that integrates Bayesian nonparametrics and max-margin learning, which are conventionally two separate paradigms and enjoy complementary advantages. We develop an efÔ¨Åcient variational algorithm for posterior inference, and our extensive empirical studies on large-scale MovieLens and EachMovie data sets appear to justify the aforementioned dual advantages. 1
