76 jmlr-2008-Optimization Techniques for Semi-Supervised Support Vector Machines


Source: pdf

Author: Olivier Chapelle, Vikas Sindhwani, Sathiya S. Keerthi

Abstract: Due to its wide applicability, the problem of semi-supervised classiÔ¨Åcation is attracting increasing attention in machine learning. Semi-Supervised Support Vector Machines (S 3 VMs) are based on applying the margin maximization principle to both labeled and unlabeled examples. Unlike SVMs, their formulation leads to a non-convex optimization problem. A suite of algorithms have recently been proposed for solving S3 VMs. This paper reviews key ideas in this literature. The performance and behavior of various S3 VM algorithms is studied together, under a common experimental setting. Keywords: semi-supervised learning, support vector machines, non-convex optimization, transductive learning
