Source: pdf

Author: Xuchen Yao ; Benjamin Van Durme ; Chris Callison-Burch ; Peter Clark

Abstract: We introduce a novel discriminative model for phrase-based monolingual alignment using a semi-Markov CRF. Our model achieves stateof-the-art alignment accuracy on two phrasebased alignment datasets (RTE and paraphrase), while doing significantly better than other strong baselines in both non-identical alignment and phrase-only alignment. Additional experiments highlight the potential benefit of our alignment model to RTE, paraphrase identification and question answering, where even a naive application of our modelâ€™s alignment score approaches the state ofthe art.
